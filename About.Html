History

The Maletsunyane Braai Festival began as a celebration of Basotho heritage and has grown into one of the most exciting tourism events in Lesotho. The festival started in 2016 as a small outdoor gathering where friends and visitors came together to braai,listen to music,and enjoy the scenery of Maletsunyane Falls. The idea was created to bring people together while showcasing the beauty of the highlands of Lesotho.Today the festival is known as "Africa's highest braai festival" because it takes place aboout 2,260 meters above sea level in the mountains of Semonkong.

Importance

-Creates business opportunities for local communities.
-Celebrate food,music, and Basotho culture.
